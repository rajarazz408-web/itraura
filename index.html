Inherited Purity.

At ItrAura, we believe that purity is not a claim—it is our inheritance. While the modern world turned to synthetic fragrances and alcohol bases, we remained anchored in the 400-year-old steam distillation traditions of Kannauj.

Our oils are birthed in Traditional Copper Degs, captured through the slow dance of steam and condensation. 100% botanical, zero synthetics, and zero alcohol. This is nature, matured to perfection in artisanal glass.
