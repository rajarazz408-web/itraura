The Sacred Alchemy

The Art of Pure Distillation

01

The Copper Deg

Pure copper stills are hand-beaten to ensure the perfect molecular catalyst. Fresh petals meet controlled fire, releasing the essence without burning its delicate soul.

02

Steam Ritual

The vapor travels through 'Chonga' (Bamboo pipes)—a 400-year-old organic conduit that preserves the botanical integrity and earthy truth of the scent.

03

Condensation

In the Bhapka, submerged in cold water, vapors transform back into liquid gold. A silent marriage of fire, water, and ancient, unhurried patience.

04

The Pure Ruh

What remains is 100% botanical oil. Alcohol-free. Synthetic-free. The absolute truth of nature captured in glass. Essence in Every Drop.
