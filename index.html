Hindustani Virasat, Global Pehchan

ItrAura koi aam perfume brand nahi hai. Yeh ek safar hai jo Kannauj ki purani galiyon se shuru hokar aapki shaksiyat tak pahunchta hai. Hum wahi 400 saal purani "Deg-Bhapka" vidhi ka upyog karte hain jahan phoolon ko tambe ke bado bartano mein dhimi aanch par pakaya jata hai.

Har ek drop mein shuddhata ka waada hai. Isme 0% Alcohol hai aur 0% Chemicals. Jab aap ItrAura lagate hain, toh aap sirf mehakte nahi hain, balki ek sadiyon purani virasat ko pehente hain. Sirf ek boond poore 12 ghante tak aapki maujoodgi ka ehsas karati rehti hai.

12-Hour Longevity Science

ItrAura ke molecules heavy aur concentrated hote hain. Alcohol based perfumes hawa mein turant ud jate hain, lekin humara Oil-Based formula skin ke pulse points par chipak jata hai aur dhire-dhire din bhar khushboo release karta hai.

Art of Slow Distillation

Ek 12ml ki bottle banane mein humein hazaro phool aur kai dinon ka sabr lagta hai. Deg-Bhapka vidhi mein Tamba (Copper) ka upyog tel ko thanda rakhne aur uski natural scent profile ko bachane ke liye kiya jata hai.

Safety & Purity Commitment

Aapki skin sabse zaroori hai. ItrAura mein hum Bina Alcohol aur Bina Synthetics ke kaam karte hain. Yeh ittar skin friendly hai aur dadi-nani ke zamane ki shuddhata ko wapas lata hai.

Client Guide

ItrAura kaise apply karein?

Bilkul nahi! ItrAura 100% botanical oil-based perfume hai. Isme na alcohol hai na hi skin-damaging chemicals.
