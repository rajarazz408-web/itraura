At ItrAura, we don't just create perfumes; we preserve a legacy. While the world moved towards alcohol and synthetics, we stayed true to the fields of Hasayan—the true rose capital of the East.

Our essences are born in family-owned distilleries, crafted using the 400-year-old Deg-Bhapka steam distillation method of Kannauj. 100% botanical, zero alcohol, and matured like fine wine. This is the ultimate luxury for the modern soul.
